Johan Riley Fyodor Taiwo Samuel is famous as the son of supermodel Heidi Klum and Grammy-award-winning singer Seal. Johan is a gamer.

His parents could not save their marriage even after eight times wedding vows renewal

His parents got engaged in December 2004 on a glacier in Whistler, British Columbia, before tying their wedding knot on a beach in Mexico in May 2005. 

His mother adopted his father’s surname, Samuel, and became Heidi Samuel. To make their marriage successful, they went on renewing their wedding vows in front of their friends and family on every anniversary. 

But unfortunately, their marriage could not last more than seven years, with Heidi filing for divorce in April 2012, three months after their divorce announcement, and restored her name from Heidi Samuel to Heidi Klum. 

According to Klum, she and her husband renewed their wedding vows annually so that they could make their marriage work thinking that it would be fun and fest of love, but despite that, it did not work.

She concluded by saying she tried.

The reason for divorce

The divorce of his parents got finalized in October 2014. When Klum was asked about the reason for their split, Klum replied, saying that she did not want to talk positively or negatively about the ups and downs of her marriage.

Still, later it was reported to be Seal’s inability to control his anger. 

The rumor of Klum’s infidelity also circulated after Seal’s statement in 2012, where he said of Klum that she could have shown some class and have waited at least until their divorce before deciding to fornicate. 

But later, he clarified, saying he was not implying his wife was cheating on him while they were together.

The custody battle got worsen with passing year

The ex-couple was co-parenting all of their kids after their divorce, but the custody battle gradually worsened with the passing year. 

Heidi, in an interview, admitted co-parenting with her ex-husband to be hard as things were not rosy between them. 

Seal even claimed that there was no teamwork from the side of Klum when it came to co-parenting. 

In 2020, Klum filed for an emergency court hearing after Seal refused Klum to take their kids to Germany with her. 

While Seal blamed Klum for having a hidden agenda in taking their children to Germany. 

Seal later justified saying he feared his children being exposed to the Covid-19 virus. 

Later, the couple reached a custody agreement allowing Klum to take their kids to Germany while Seal was given an expanded time with them before and after they went.

His mother finds him and his brother sexy

Johan has an older brother Henry Gunther Ademola Dashtu Samuel, 16, and a younger sister,Lou Sulola Samuel, 12, born to his parents, Heidi and Seal. 

His mother was full of praise while talking about them. She said that Johan resembles his dad, saying his dad is very hot and handsome. 

At the same time, she compared Johan’s look to a European soccer player and told of having gorgeous mixed skin with beautiful big lips and wild, big hair.

Having said that, she further added saying that they have rocking bodies and feared that they have to fend off the ladies lining around her house as they grow older.

Johan has an elder half-sister Leni Olumi Klum born to his mother from her first marriage to stylist Flavio Briatore.

Relationship with his step-dad

His mother first met his step-dad guitarist and songwriter, Tom Kaulitz, in 2018. After making their first appearance at a jewelry designer’s party, they started making frequent appearances together. 

They dated for eight months before Kaulitz popped the question with a teal sapphire ring and eventually married in February 2019. Kaulitz is 17 years younger than Klum.

Kaulitz has proved to be a good husband as well as an excellent step-dad to his four teenage stepkids. In 2019, Kaulitz said he has been giving guitar lessons to his stepchildren.

Talking to the US daily in January 2022, Klum admitted to not being an easy job to step into an immediate family, having all four kids and teenagers. 

She added, saying that they were easier when they were younger and admitted every age comes with its’ challenges. 

She gushed about her spouse’s parenting skills and said that he has been teaching Henry to drive after having taught Leni.
